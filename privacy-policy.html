Privacy Policy

Overview

DiaTask is a voice‑powered task management application that allows users to create tasks through speech. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

Information Collected

Voice Recordings and Transcriptions: When a user taps the microphone and speaks a task, the spoken audio is captured and sent to Apple's iOS Speech Recognition service to convert the audio into text. The raw audio is deleted once transcription is complete.

Task Details: Transcribed text is sent to the OpenAI API to extract structured information such as task titles, dates/times, and categories. The resulting task data is stored in a database until the user deletes it.

Identifiers and Notifications: A device or temporary user identifier and push notification token are stored to deliver reminders. Minimal diagnostic data (such as request timing and token counts) is collected for reliability and abuse prevention.

Account Information: If a user signs up with an email and password or through another sign‑in provider, the information required to create and manage an account (using Firebase Authentication) is collected.

Use of Information

Task Creation: Voice is transcribed to text, parsed to extract task details, and used to create task entries with automatic reminders (default: 10 minutes before the scheduled time).

Notifications: Device tokens enable scheduled local push notifications reminding users of upcoming tasks.

Application Functionality and Support: Diagnostic data helps maintain reliability, troubleshoot issues, and prevent misuse.

Cross‑Device Sync: Task data is stored in Firebase Firestore to ensure synchronization across devices.

DiaTask does not sell data or use it for advertising.

Sharing and Processing

Processors: Service providers are used only to operate DiaTask. These include:

  • Apple's iOS Speech Recognition for on‑device or cloud transcription.
  • OpenAI for text parsing and task extraction.
  • Firebase (Google) for authentication and cloud storage.
  • Push notification services such as Apple Push Notification Service.

Policies of Third Parties: OpenAI's API does not use customer API data to train models by default. Users should refer to OpenAI's service agreements and usage policies for more details.

No Third‑Party Advertising: Data is not shared with advertisers. Content is shared only as needed to operate or improve the service, prevent abuse, or comply with legal requirements.

Retention and Deletion

Voice Recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription is completed.

Tasks: Persist until deleted by the user. Users may delete tasks individually or use a "Delete All Data" option within settings.

Diagnostics: Retained only as long as necessary to maintain reliability and prevent misuse.

Accounts: Deleting an account removes the associated task data and authentication details.

Security

Data is protected in transit with TLS (HTTPS) and stored with access controls. While these measures enhance security, no system is entirely secure.

International Transfers

DiaTask operates in the United States and may process data on servers in other countries where service providers operate. Measures are taken to ensure an adequate level of data protection.

Children

The service is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable). Personal data from children is not knowingly collected. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided data, they may contact DiaTask to request deletion.

User Choices

Permissions: Users may disable microphone access and notifications via device settings.

Data Deletion: Users may delete individual tasks or use the "Delete All Data" option in settings. Users may also request deletion or correction of personal data by contacting support.

AI Disclaimer: AI‑generated outputs may be inaccurate or incomplete; users should not rely on DiaTask for medical, legal, or other safety‑critical purposes.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Material changes will be communicated via the app or by email. Continued use of DiaTask after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or data practices, please email support@voxtask.info.
