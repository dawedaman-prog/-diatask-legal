Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Audio you choose to transcribe, reminder titles, notes, dates, and settings.
  • Anonymous identifiers (e.g., device or temporary user ID) and push tokens.
  • Minimal diagnostics for reliability (e.g., error logs).

How We Use Information

  • Transcribe your audio and parse reminders.
  • Schedule notifications and provide app functionality.
  • Improve reliability and support.

Sharing and Processing

  • We use trusted processors, including OpenAI (API) for transcription and reminder parsing, and push notification providers.
  • Content you choose to process (e.g., audio, derived text) is sent to these providers solely to provide the service.
  • OpenAI's API does not use customer API data to train their models by default; see OpenAI's Services Agreement and Policies for details.

AI Processing Notice

  • AI outputs may be inaccurate or incomplete. Do not rely on the app for medical, legal, or other safety-critical decisions.
  • Please avoid submitting sensitive personal data (e.g., health, children's personal data, financial account numbers) unless strictly necessary for your use.

Retention and Deletion

  • Audio is processed securely and not retained longer than necessary for transcription.
  • We record minimal usage events (e.g., request timing, token counts) for reliability, abuse prevention, and billing.
  • Reminders persist until you delete them. You can request deletion in Settings ("Delete All Data") or by contacting us.

International Transfers

  • We operate in the United States and may process data in other countries where our processors operate. We take steps to protect your data consistent with applicable laws.

Security

  • Data is protected in transit with TLS and stored with access controls.

Children

  • The service is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 where applicable). We do not knowingly collect children's personal data.

Your Choices

  • Control microphone access and notifications in system settings.
  • In the app, you can delete your data in Settings. You may also contact us to access or correct your information.
